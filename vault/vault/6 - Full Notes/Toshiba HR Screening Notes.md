**Digital Solutions (IoT/AI/Big Data):** This is where modern software development is heavily leveraged. Engineers work on **IoT (Internet of Things) platforms** like their **SPINEX** suite, **cloud-based data collection and analysis systems**, and services that integrate **AI/ML (Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning)** for advanced analytics, predictive maintenance, and efficiency improvements. They develop the backend logic (Java, Spring Boot, Node.js), databases, and visualization services (ReactJS, Angular) often hosted on **AWS** or **Azure**.

**Retail/Global Commerce Solutions:** Toshiba is a major provider of Point-of-Sale (POS) and retail solutions. Their **ELERA** platform, built on a **microservices architecture**, uses AI and IoT to manage everything from self-checkout systems to inventory and in-store automation.

**Elevate Sky** is a cloud-based suite of services primarily focused on **Document Solutions** and **Managed Print Services** for businesses. As an entry-level software engineer, you could be working on:
**Cloud Applications:** Developing and integrating software-as-a-service (SaaS) features for printing, document capture, and workflow management.
**Workflow Automation:** Building the logic behind features like **Elevate Sky Workflow**, which uses **AI/LLMs** (Large Language Models) to intelligently capture, process, and route documents, automating tedious office tasks.
**Cloud Integration:** Ensuring seamless and secure connectivity with popular third-party cloud services like **Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Box, and Dropbox**.
